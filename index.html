How It Works

Our process is designed to ensure transparency, punctuality, and smooth coordination.

1

Find Work

Browse all event helper and part-time job opportunities, including venue, date, time, and dress code details.

2

Get Selected

Shortlisted Genies receive a verification call based on profile completion, skills, and reliability.

3

Join WhatsApp Group

Selected Genies are added to a dedicated group for real-time coordination before and during the event.

4

Task Prep & Check-in

Check in via the app 1 hour before and reach the venue 30 mins early. Follow coordinator instructions.

5

During the Task

Maintain discipline, follow guidelines, and perform responsibilities. Professionalism increases future eligibility.

6

Check-out & Payment

Check out via the app. Payment is securely processed within 7 days through Cashfree via bank or UPI.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers about working with Genie Workforce.

How much can I earn from weekend event jobs in Pune?

+

Earnings depend on the role and duration. Short weekend jobs in Pune can pay for a few hours of work, while longer event staff assignments may offer higher pay. Skilled tasks like stall management or promotional staff generally provide better payouts.

Are there opportunities for temporary event staff in Pune besides weekends?

+

Yes, we have opportunities throughout the week depending on the events scheduled, including exhibitions, marathons, and corporate events.

How do I apply for event staff jobs in Pune?

+

Simply sign up on our website or app, complete your profile, browse available tasks, and apply for the ones that fit your schedule.

Do I need prior experience for weekend part-time jobs?

+

No prior experience is necessary for many helper roles. We welcome students and freshers. However, specific skilled roles may require some background experience.

How and when will I receive my payment?

+

Payments are processed within 7 days of task completion through Cashfree and can be redeemed directly to your bank account or via UPI.
